First Class 1

The Tour and the Golden Question

Hook 0:00 to 0:12

A new person walks into your gym.

What you do in the next five minutes decides everything. Whether they come back. Whether they buy a membership. Whether you ever see them again.

The Basics 0:12 to 0:40

Most trainers know the basics. Show them the room. Show them the machines. Show them where the toilets are, where to fill their water, where to drop their stuff.

That's the tour. That's fine. That's a must.

But here's the thing. The basics are not enough. If all you do is point at stuff, they will start thinking: what do I need you for? AI can do it.

And what we're selling here is not a gym. We're selling an experience.

You Are The Vibe 0:40 to 1:05

So before showing them around, do this. Walk up to them. Smile. Introduce yourself properly.

"Hey, I'm Jino. Nice to meet you. Welcome!"

Look them in the eye. Mean it. Because in the first 30 seconds, they are already deciding if this place feels good or if it feels weird.

You are the vibe. You are the first impression.

The Golden Question 1:05 to 1:45

Now here's where most trainers stop. They tour, they smile, they leave. Don't.

Ask the number one golden question.

"Have you done this sort of exercise before?"

One sentence. And it unlocks everything. Because the answer tells you exactly who is standing in front of you. A complete beginner. Or an experienced athlete.

And those two people? They need two completely different experiences from you today. Same room, same class, different play.

So write that question down. Use it on the next new member who walks in. Then you're going to tell me their response.

In the next video, I'll break down exactly what to do if their answer is "no, I've never done this before." The beginner flowchart. The exact moves. See you then.
