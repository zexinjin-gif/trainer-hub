Coaching Philosophy

The Full Read

A lot of people think being a good coach is about knowing every exercise, every rep, every set.

And yeah, that stuff matters.

But here at F45 Crows Nest, our coaching philosophy is pretty simple.

We coach the person. Not the workout.

The Workout Is The Workout

The workout is on the screen. It's the same workout at every F45 around the world.

So what makes a difference? It's us. The coaches on the floor.

Make Them Feel Seen

When someone walks through that door, I want them to feel like they belong here.

Not just another number. Not just another body in the room.

We know your name. We know if something's been bothering your shoulder. We know if you had a rough week and you still showed up anyway.

That matters to us.

Meet Them Where They Are

Our job isn't to just count your reps and tell you to go harder.

Our job is to meet you where you are.

If that means modifying an exercise so it works for your body, we do that.

If that means giving you a push because we know you've got more in the tank, we do that too.

Every person on that floor is different. Different goals. Different experience. Different story.

And we coach to that.

The Community

The best part about this place is the people.

When you walk in, you're not just joining a gym. You're joining a community.

People cheer each other on. They notice when someone's missing. They celebrate each other's wins.

And as coaches, we set that tone. We bring the energy. We make sure every single person in that room feels seen.

What Makes Us Special

So yeah, the workouts are incredible. The programming is world class.

But what makes F45 Crows Nest special?

It's that we actually care about the people in front of us.

That's our coaching philosophy. That's what we're about.
